about

Dave Hawkins writes songs about trying to stay present in a world that won't stop moving. From his basement in Denver, he blends indie rock, synth textures, and quiet reflection into something steady and human.

His new EP Half Alive looks at the loops we get stuck in and the moments that break us out. From the constant hum of screens to the quiet clarity that follows when they finally go dark. Written between early mornings and late nights at home, the songs find meaning in the blur: small flashes of humor, hope, and resistance inside everyday life.

It's music for the middle ground, not quite optimistic, not quite cynical, just honest about how strange it feels to be alive right now.
